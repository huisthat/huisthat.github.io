# Introduction
I've always wanted to write a book, maybe because I was influenced by my sister. The book will be named **Record**, as its name suggests,it is a record about my junior college life, which has been one of the best times of my life. 

I will only be posting part one of the book which comprises of 7 chapters, since part two will comprise of some secrets that I am not comfortable with sharing. Part three will be about my friends, but I have yet to start on it, so it will take quite some time to be uploaded.



## Author’s note:

This book is a recount of my junior college times, and it may not be as interesting as it seems, but it acts as a memory keepsake for those good times. 

Some things may not be as interesting as you think, and there might be no link between them because human nature is not as logical as what novels portray it. I’ve added my personal touch and emotions to the things that happened that many do not know. 

I originally meant to write it in chinese, but I realised that it is very difficult, almost impossible, to translate all the nitty gritty details into chinese such as jokes and the knowledge we learn in school.
