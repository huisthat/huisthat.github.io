Introduction

This was a pair project done over the course of three months, It was rewarding and fulfilling, as I learnt to start and complete a software development project from scratch.

Project Introduction

ModPal is a graphical visualization tool for module planning. Our dynamic maps model the connections among modules in NUS, so university students can get a big picture of their academic roadmap, and visualize and explore what its curriculum coverage really means. It also provides a study planner with a requirement verifier for students to plan their modules more effectively.

It currently supports the major and specializations for Business Analytics, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Economics and Information Systems, for the cohort of 20/21.

Challenges

It was my first time using React, and wow, it was really tough at the start. The different React components were extremely confusing, especially for a beginner. With much experimenting and googling, it looked more and more like our prototype on Figma yay!

Another thing was that we decided to take away the login/signup and backend part for this project, as it seems unneccessary for the target user to be able to view the different majors and specializations.
