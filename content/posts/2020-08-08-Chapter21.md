Chapter 21: Ending

After the graduation ceremony, A Levels flew by in a blink of an eye. Everyday was just repeating the cycle of studying. It was the period where you feel tired of studying, yet you feel fulfilled by that only goal at that time. 

There was a barbeque class gathering at Jiankai’s house a week after A Levels ended. I really miss that day. It was where we were all so carefree, bathing in the happiness of the end of A Levels, and we knew one another well enough to have so much fun. 

The most memorable part was when I went home that day. We wrote one another graduation/farewell cards, and I welled up in tears after reading the cards I received at night. I am really glad to have been in this class, where I can click well with so many people.
