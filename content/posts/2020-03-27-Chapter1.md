“Last but not least, let’s relive the fun moments with 18S7J,” the emcee said. The screen lit up. In the video, 23 students ran into the central plaza, coming together to take a photo. Watching these bright-eyed students, Ee Ting’s voice sounded in the background, “This is S7J, and we are quite a peculiar bunch! Let me show you around…” As we got ready to go up the stage in the duration of the montage, we laughed at the funny details in the video, such as Bernard and Ziqi pressing the lift doors frantically, saying at the same time, “Faster, faster!” “I want to eat my ban mian!”. Memories slowly filled my brain as the video ended, and the emcee read out our names one by one to receive the graduation bear……
